About Surv&Earn

Empowering voices, rewarding opinions, and bridging the gap between businesses and consumers across Nepal.

Our Story

Surv&Earn is Nepal's premier survey platform that connects businesses with consumers through meaningful market research while ensuring participants are fairly compensated for their valuable time and insights.

Founded with the vision of democratizing market research in Nepal, we believe that every opinion matters and deserves recognition. Our platform serves as a bridge between companies seeking authentic consumer feedback and individuals who want to share their perspectives while earning rewards.

We understand the unique cultural and economic landscape of Nepal, which is why our surveys are designed to be culturally relevant, accessible in local languages, and respectful of our users' time. Whether you're a student looking to earn pocket money, a professional seeking additional income, or simply someone who enjoys sharing opinions, Surv&Earn provides a trusted platform for meaningful participation.

Our commitment extends beyond just facilitating surveys. We're building a community where voices are heard, opinions are valued, and participation is rewarded. Through transparent processes, timely payments, and user-friendly interfaces, we're revolutionizing how market research is conducted in Nepal.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable ecosystem where businesses gain valuable consumer insights while individuals are fairly compensated for their participation, fostering economic empowerment and informed decision-making across Nepal.

Our Vision

To become the leading survey platform in South Asia, recognized for our commitment to user satisfaction, data integrity, and social impact, while setting new standards for ethical market research practices.
